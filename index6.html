Euro 2020: When is the tournament and who has qualified?

Teams are booking their Euro 2020 spots at the business end of the qualifying campaign but with 24 sides set to play across 12 host cities, how will the tournament work?

The top two teams from each of the 10 groups qualify for Euro 2020, while the four remaining positions will be decided in the play-offs in March 2020.

Qualifying for UEFA EURO 2020 remains largely the same (with 24 finalists in all), although the European Qualifiers started later than normal – the March after a major tournament instead of immediately in September (i.e. March 2019 rather than September 2018 in this case). No team gained automatic qualification as hosts (there are 12 host nations in all).
